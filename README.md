# Welcome to The Serpent's Eye tavern, step right in!

Welcome to the modpack of The Serpent's Eye, the ~~tavern~~ community of the variety-streamer Helia (@sunofsnek). What started as a loose collection of mods for a medieval fantasy vibe quickly grew into a proper pack that we wanted to share with everyone.

We know what we'll be doing - playing with spells and build a cozy tavern - but what you do with this is entirely your choice!

# What to expect here

You find yourself in a magical land, unburdened by quests, and only bound to your free will. Unleash your creativity in building your world and fill it with life, be it as a farmer, tavernkeep, or magician.
